About Sri Lanka

An island nation with over 2,500 years of history, Sri Lanka is a vibrant tapestry of cultures, landscapes, and traditions. Located in the Indian Ocean, it offers a unique blend of natural beauty, rich heritage, and diverse experiences.

Geography

  • 🏞️ Area: 65,610 sq km
  • 🌴 Landscape: Tropical rainforests, mountains, beaches
  • 🍃 Famous for: Tea plantations, national parks

Quick Facts

  • 🏛️ Capital: Sri Jayawardenepura Kotte
  • 👥 Population: ~22 million
  • 🗣️ Languages: Sinhala, Tamil
  • 💰 Currency: Sri Lankan Rupee

Cultural Mosaic

A multicultural society with Buddhism at its core, Sri Lanka embraces Sinhalese, Tamil, Muslim, and other ethnic groups. Despite historical challenges, the country has emerged as a resilient and welcoming destination.
